# Purpose

This document defines the strategic vision, design philosophy and guiding principles of AI Product Operating System.

It serves as the foundation for all future architectural and implementation decisions.

---

# Vision

AI Product OS is an AI-native environment for designing, building, testing and evolving AI products.

Unlike traditional product management software, the platform is designed around collaboration with AI rather than manual documentation.

The user starts with an idea.

The system finishes with a production-ready AI product.

---

# Problem Statement

Today, AI Product Managers manually perform dozens of repetitive tasks:

- Writing product documentation
- Creating Product Requirement Documents
- Defining architecture
- Designing prompts
- Building AI pipelines
- Running tests
- Comparing results
- Maintaining documentation

These activities consume significant time while providing limited strategic value.

The platform should automate these activities.

---

# Product Vision

The platform behaves as an AI company inside a single application.

Instead of isolated assistants, users collaborate with an orchestrated AI team capable of performing the work of multiple specialists.

```text
User
  │
  ▼
AI Orchestrator
  ├── Product Manager
  ├── Business Analyst
  ├── Solution Architect
  ├── Prompt Engineer
  ├── AI Engineer
  ├── QA Engineer
  ├── Technical Reviewer
  └── Knowledge Manager
```

---

# Core Design Principles

## AI First

Every workflow begins with AI.

Manual editing is always optional.

## Conversation Before Forms

Users describe ideas naturally.

AI converts conversations into structured artifacts.

## Progressive Refinement

The platform never asks users to complete large forms.

Instead, AI continuously improves product artifacts through dialogue.

## Everything is Versioned

Every significant artifact must support version history:

- Products
- Prompts
- Pipelines
- Test Suites
- Datasets
- Architecture
- Documentation

The platform should achieve:

|--------|--------|
| Product Canvas generated by AI | >80% |

| Automatic pipeline generation | >90% |
| Product readiness visibility | 100% |

---

# Out of Scope

The platform is not intended to replace:

- Source code IDEs
- Project management systems
- CI/CD platforms

Instead, it integrates with them.
