# AI Product Operating System (AI Product OS)

> Enterprise AI platform for designing, developing, testing, evaluating and continuously improving AI-powered products.

---

# Vision

AI Product OS is not another product management tool.

It is an operating system for building AI products.

Instead of manually creating documents, prompts, pipelines and tests, Product Managers collaborate with an AI team that performs most of the engineering and product design work.

The platform transforms a simple product idea into a production-ready AI system.

---

# Mission

Reduce AI product development time by more than **80%** while improving product quality through AI-assisted design, prompt engineering, automated testing, continuous evaluation and organizational knowledge accumulation.

---

# Guiding Principles

## AI First

Every workflow starts with AI assistance.

Manual editing is optional.

Never force users to fill large forms.

## Conversation Driven

Users communicate naturally.

AI generates structured artifacts.

## Everything Is Versioned

- Products
- Prompts
- Pipelines
- Schemas
- Test datasets
- Metrics
- Documentation

Every artifact has complete version history.

## Continuous Evaluation

Every prompt or pipeline change automatically creates a new evaluation cycle.

## AI Collaboration

The platform behaves like a multidisciplinary AI team:

- AI Product Manager
- Business Analyst
- Solution Architect
- Prompt Engineer
- AI Engineer
- QA Engineer
- Technical Reviewer

# Success Criteria

- More than 80% of Product Canvas is AI-generated.
- More than 90% of prompts are created through Prompt Studio.
- Every product has complete version history.
- Every prompt change is automatically tested.
- Every AI product has measurable maturity.
- Every experiment becomes searchable organizational knowledge.
- Product Managers spend more time making decisions than writing documentation.

# Long-term Vision

AI Product OS should become the single operating environment for designing, developing, testing, evaluating and continuously improving AI products across the organization.
